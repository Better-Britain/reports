Broken Britain Briefing

After decades of neglect, many people feel Britain is broken. We often slip into cycles of outrage and reaction, and some fear we have moved from Broken Britain to an unfixable Britain. We want to test that claim against evidence and scrutiny. The Broken Britain Briefing is a collection of papers examining the real state of the country and what is being done to fix it, aiming to reduce radical responses with cool, calm, structured and clear reports on the problems facing Britain today.
